Sobre Nosotros

¿Quienes Somos?

eghhhy dsfghhrr is a family-owned business located in El Sargento, BCS, Mexico.

eedsfghy, the designer and manufacturer of all the guns we make. I have a diverse background in the spearfishing world growing up in Northern California and starting this sport at 8 yrs old in 1966 with my father and his dive club named the Scuba Knights my beginnings were on Scuba in the deep cold waters of Monterey and Carmel Bay's.

I entered my first Cen Cal tournament in 1974 and with an introduction to tournaments progressed in National and Individual Tournaments like the Bluewater Water World Cup that shaped my life to this day.

I moved to Baja California in 2015 and along with my Precision Machining background and my Fathers woodworking skills started to work on our gun ideas. Taking the things I liked about different brands I used over the years and incorporating them into our vision we developed our current designs.
